Think about how you will use the results

The Coach will help you think about how the answer to your research question will inform the decisions you make regarding your technology.

When making decisions, it is important to think about the costs of the technology you're considering and the risks associated with your decision. The following 4 steps will help you think about that.

For example, you may be trying to decide:

  • Whether to keep paying for a software tool you're already using
  • Whether to buy licenses for a tool you're piloting on a trial basis
  • Whether to change how teachers and/or students use the tool, based on a new approach tested in your evaluation

Sections A through D will help you think about these questions. Remember, you can always come back to this step and change your answers. In other words, nothing you enter here is permanent, so just fill this out as best you can.
