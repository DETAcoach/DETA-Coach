The Basics

These first questions will help the Coach make sure you have what you need to get started and get you thinking about the goal of your Ed Tech evaluation.

Have you selected an educational technology for your evaluation?

In order to use the Ed Tech Coach you need to have an educational technology to evaluate. The Coach is not designed to help you select or compare potential technologies to evaluate, but you can refer to our brief guide – Choose a technology – if you have not yet selected a technology to evaluate.

What is the name of the educational technology you are testing?

What is the outcome you hope to change by using the technology?

Things to Consider

An outcome is knowledge, skills, attitudes, or other desired benefits that are attained as a result of an activity (e.g. engaging with the educational technology). We encourage you to meet with your team and determine the outcome you are most interested in measuring during your evaluation.

Example

  • Student academic achievement: end-of-year math test scores
  • Student non-academic achievement: attendance

Who are or will be your technology users?

Things to Consider

The technology users are the group that is directly engaging with the technology. In some cases this may not be the same group for which you are trying to improve outcomes (for example you may hope a learning management platform used by teachers to coordinate lessons and assignments will improve student outcomes).

Great! You are now ready to begin designing your research approach.

You will need to determine what outcomes you’ll be measuring before you can continue.

To measure a technology’s effectiveness it is critical to know what you are hoping to accomplish from its use. Therefore, you will need to determine what outcomes you’ll be measuring before you can continue. We encourage you to talk with your colleagues about possible outcomes you would like to improve. This could be based on your priorities or what you think the technology could reasonably achieve. Come back to the Coach when you know what outcome you wish to target.

Without educational technology to evaluate you will not be able to proceed through the Coach

We have put together the guide Choose a technology to help you make that selection. Once you’ve made your selection, please return to the Coach and continue your evaluation.

Things to Consider

You can always come back and revise your answers here and the Coach will revisit some of these questions in other tools. We encourage you to talk with your colleagues about your research goals and make revisions if necessary.
