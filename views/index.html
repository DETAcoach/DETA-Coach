Is your Educational Technology moving the needle in the classroom?

The Ed Tech RCE (Rapid Cycle Evaluation) Coach helps you evaluate educational technology use in your school so that you can make better and more informed decisions about how it can best be used to improved student outcomes.

GET STARTED

Testing your tech is more important than you think.

The Ed Tech RCE (Rapid Cycle Evaluation) Coach is a powerful tool that helps you collect and analyze your own data to answer important questions such as:

  • Does your ed tech program lead to the desired outcomes?
  • Is your software tool cost effective?
  • Should you buy the software you're pilot testing?

Proper testing will get you the right answers, more quickly and easily.

Welcome to the beta version of the Ed Tech RCE Coach!

We’re excited to get your feedback on the support and tools the Coach provides to help you conduct your own rapid cycle evaluations of education technologies. At the top of each screen there’s a button that you can click to provide feedback on that screen. You’ll need to create an account to provide feedback there, and you can also email us at EdTechRCE@mathematica-mpr.com.

The Coach currently provides support for conducting RCEs using a matched comparison group design. You can use this method to evaluate products already in use, as long as you can identify some similar students, teachers or schools that didn’t use the product.

Coming soon: We’ll be adding more tools in the coming months to support forward-looking pilots of new technologies or implementation strategies. We’re also looking for partners who want to evaluate products that support teacher productivity, professional development or non-academic student outcomes. Let us know what else you’d like to see!
